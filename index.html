Muscle Bioprinting Services

01

Volumetric Muscle Loss Repair

Aligned myofiber scaffolds for VML defect treatment. Anisotropic fibrin-gelatin bioinks printed in uniaxial layers to guide myoblast alignment and fusion. Neovascularization channels integrated for thick-tissue viability.

02

Actuator & Soft Robotics Research

Muscle-powered biohybrid actuators for soft robotics and prosthetics. Printed myoblast-laden scaffolds cultured to functional myotubes that contract on electrical stimulation. Custom geometry for force-displacement optimization.

03

Cultivated Meat Scaffolds

Food-grade scaffolding for cultured meat production. Edible biomaterial scaffolds structuring bovine or porcine myosatellite cells into structured meat textures. GRAs-aligned material selection for regulatory compliance.

BIOMATERIALS

Muscle Scaffold Materials

Muscle is the body's primary actuator. Our scaffold materials mimic the mechanical anisotropy and biochemical environment that guide satellite cell differentiation.

  • ● Fibrin-gelatin bioink — native muscle ECM analog
  • ● GelMA — photocrosslinkable, tunable 8–20 kPa stiffness
  • ● Collagen type I — myoblast adhesion and alignment signal
  • ● Matrigel for primary satellite cell maintenance
  • ● Edible alginate-pectin for cultivated meat applications
  • ● Electrically conductive PEDOT:PSS composite scaffolds
